AIR HOCKEY

In this software, Ryan and I developed a a virtual version of air hockey. This air hockey game comes with a bonus map version which provides a Soccer Version aswell! This game can be played either single player or multiplayer. In single player, the player uses the mouse to hit the Hockeypuck/Soccerball andfaces against a CPU in either Hockey or Soccer. In the other hand, if the player decides to play multiplayer there will be two sets of controls. Player 1 control's is the mouse itself; player 2 control's is the up, down, left, right controls.
